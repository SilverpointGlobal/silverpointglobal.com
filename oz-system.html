Intelligence Architecture

Six agents.
One unified intelligence.

Oz is not a tool. It is an operating system for revenue — a planetary-scale intelligence network that monitors, predicts, and captures economic opportunity in real time.

The Agent Network

Six autonomous agents.
Always running.

Agent 01
Signal Agent
Economic Signal Acquisition

Continuously scans billions of global economic data points — news, filings, hiring patterns, funding events, partnerships, and behavioral signals — to detect emerging opportunities at the edge of visibility.

Agent 02
Research Agent
Deep Intelligence Synthesis

Takes raw signals and builds dense intelligence profiles on companies, founders, industries, and markets. Surfaces the context needed to convert a signal into a qualified opportunity.

Agent 03
Campaign Agent
Strategic Campaign Execution

Designs and executes precision outreach campaigns tailored to each opportunity profile. Adapts messaging, timing, and sequencing based on real-time intelligence feedback.

Agent 04
Enrichment Agent
Contact & Data Enrichment

Fills every intelligence gap — contact data, organizational structure, decision-maker mapping, technology stack, intent signals — so every outreach arrives fully informed.

Agent 05
Outreach Agent
Intelligent Engagement

Deploys multi-channel outreach with intelligence-driven personalization. Monitors response patterns and adapts sequences in real time to maximize engagement velocity.

Agent 06
Analytics Agent
Intelligence & Learning Engine

Measures performance across every dimension, feeds learning back into the system, and continuously improves signal quality, opportunity scoring, and conversion rates over time.

System Architecture

The Intelligence Core.

All six agents are coordinated by the Oz Intelligence Core — a central orchestration layer that routes signals, resolves conflicts, prioritizes opportunities, and maintains the full intelligence context of your revenue ecosystem.

The Oz Process

From signal to close.

01
Signal Detection

Oz detects a weak economic signal — a company growing fast, a market shifting, a buyer entering a decision window.

02
Intelligence Build

Research and Enrichment agents build a dense profile — company, contacts, context, timing, and competitive landscape.

03
Opportunity Score

The Intelligence Core scores and prioritizes — routing only the highest-probability opportunities to your pipeline.

04
Engagement

Campaign and Outreach agents execute precision engagement with full context, optimal timing, and adaptive sequencing.

05
Revenue Capture

Deal intelligence guides every stage to close. Analytics agent feeds learning back, improving the entire cycle.
